Internship Program

The MGP Internship Program is a program developed to allow students to gain insight into various aspects of the business (with a specific focus on Engineering, Supply Chain, Sales/Marketing, Accounting/Finance, Research, Development & Innovation, and Corporate Relations departments), learn from the ground up, gain exposure to senior management, and develop leadership skills and true business experience.

At the end of the internship program, MGP will host its annual Internship Symposium. These students are expected to present their key learning’s and project results to the executive team consisting of CEO, CFO and Vice Presidents of each business unit.
