Working at MGP

MGP provides a wide range of promising, exciting and fulfilling career opportunities as well as competitive compensation and benefit programs.

We encourage our employees to participate in numerous professional development opportunities both internally and externally. We believe in maintaining a diverse workforce in an environment that fosters mutual respect. Most importantly we encourage our employees to maintain healthy/work-life balance.

Our Core Values Include:

  • People
  • Winning Together
  • Shared Vision of Success
  • Collaborate for Clear Results
  • Reward and Recognize
  • Pride
  • Lead with Passion
  • Be Proactive and Accountable
  • Think Outside of Your Job Description
  • Bring Innovation to Everything You Do
  • Progress
  • Develop Yourself and Your People
  • Create an Environment Where People Strive
  • Honor Your Commitments
  • Take Intelligent Risks

Company Benefits

We are continually looking for ways to make MGP a great place to work. One way is by offering a comprehensive benefits package that is designed to address your health care and financial needs, both now and into retirement. It is our goal to help MGP employees to live life well.

  • Dental
  • Vision
  • Life Insurance
  • Short and Long Term Disability
  • Flex Spending
  • 401k Plan
  • Vacation

  • Stock Purchase Plan
  • Employee Assistance Program
  • Wellness Program
